# Project Naming Ideas: "Web3 Guard" Rebrand
**Goal:** Find a premium-sounding name that reflects a professional Web3 smart contract auditing platform, while rhyming with, sounding similar to, or feeling native to the Stellar/Soroban ecosystem.

---

### 1. The "Microscope & Analysis" Vibe (Pure Auditing Focus)
*   **StellarScope** – "Scope" implies putting the code under a microscope to find vulnerabilities. It sounds highly analytical and professional.
*   **SoroScope** – Similar to above, but hyper-focused on the Soroban smart contract language.
*   **Stellalyze** (Stellar + Analyze) – Sounds like a proprietary static analysis engine built specifically for this ecosystem.
*   **LumenLogic** – Smart contracts are just business logic. "LumenLogic" implies you are auditing and verifying the core logic of the blockchain.

### 2. The "Certification & Assurance" Vibe (Enterprise Auditing Firm Focus)
*   **CertiStellar** – A direct nod to "Certify" and "Stellar". It gives off the exact same premium auditing vibe as industry giants like *CertiK*. 
*   **StellAssure** – A blend of Stellar + Assurance. In traditional software, auditing is part of "Quality Assurance". This sounds like an enterprise-grade auditing firm.
*   **StellAudit** – Clean, undeniable, and tells the user exactly what you do without any fluff. 

### 3. The "Rhyming" Vibe
*   **TrustTeller** (Rhymes with Stellar) – In banking and code, a "teller" is someone who counts, verifies, and reports the truth. It implies a platform that tells you if you can trust the code.
*   **CodeTeller** (Rhymes with Stellar) – Similar to the above, focused on verifying the code.
*   **SafeTeller** – Rhymes with Stellar and implies a system that "tells" you if a contract is safe.

### 4. Direct Play on "Stellar" (Security Focus)
*   **StellArmor** (Stellar + Armor) – Very premium, rolls off the tongue, and instantly tells users it's a security product for Stellar.
*   **Stellar Sentinel** – Strong alliteration. "Sentinel" sounds much higher-end than "Guard" and implies an autonomous, always-watching protector.

### 5. Native Ecosystem Names (Familiar to Stellar Devs)
*   **LumenScan** or **LumenSec** – XLM are called "Lumens." Naming the project after the native asset instantly proves you are building specifically for this community (similar to Etherscan). 
*   **Soroban Sentinel** – A great alliteration that sounds like an official, high-grade security oracle.
*   **Horizon Shield** – "Horizon" is the name of Stellar's official API. Calling it Horizon Shield implies deep, native integration with the network's data layer.
